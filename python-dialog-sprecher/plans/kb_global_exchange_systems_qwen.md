### Short Judgment on the User's Idea  
The idea of using the **Hotel** as an exchange system is a solid starting point for A1 learners. It aligns well with the **global ability** of basic communication in familiar, predictable situations. Other KB systems like **K045 (Hotel)**, **K049 (Schule)**, **K047 (Kursanmeldung)**, and **K052 (Persönliche Kommunikation)** share similar patterns and can be used to build a structured, thematic classroom exchange system.

---

### Global Patterns (Globale Fähigkeiten)  
These are the overarching language abilities expected at A1 level:

1. **Basic communication in familiar, predictable situations**  
2. **Use of simple, memorized expressions and sentence structures**  
3. **Limited interaction with support from the interlocutor**  
4. **Recognition of speech acts through intonation**  
5. **Use of gestures and repetition to clarify meaning**  
6. **Writing short, simple texts with personal and everyday information**  
7. **Understanding short, clear spoken and written texts**  
8. **Use of simple connectors and vocabulary in speech and writing**
